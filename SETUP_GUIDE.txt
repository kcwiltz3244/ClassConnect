INSTALLATION
1. Open config.js.
2. Paste your current Apps Script /exec URL.
3. Upload these files to GitHub:
   index.html
   styles.css
   app.js
   config.js
   manifest.webmanifest
   service-worker.js
   icon.svg
4. Commit the update.
5. Netlify will publish it automatically.

BACKEND
