How ClassConnect Works

A simple, phone-friendly guide to the features that help our class reconnect, share, celebrate, and remember.

ClassConnect is our living yearbook.

Use this guide whenever you need help finding a feature or understanding what it does. Tap any heading below to open its description.

🏠 Welcome & Home 📖 Stories & People 💬 Community 📱 Install the App

Welcome and Home

The main starting point for ClassConnect.

Why I Created ClassConnect

The welcome screen explains why Kevin created ClassConnect after the 50th reunion and invites classmates to continue the journey together.

Home Page

The Home page is arranged for phone use. It guides classmates through the class story, adding their own story, and then the other important features.

From Then to Now

Opens Our Journey and connects where the class began with where classmates are now.

Add My Story

Lets a classmate add a personal page to the living yearbook.

  • Add a biography, career, memories, city and state, birthday, and optional contact details.
  • Contact information is shown only when the classmate chooses to share it.
Edit My Story

Search for your name, select it, and enter the email connected to your story. Once verified, your existing information opens so you can update it without creating a duplicate.

Classmates, Stories, and Contact Information

Find a Classmate

Search by name to quickly locate a classmate’s living yearbook profile.

Where Are They Now?

Browse the stories, photos, memories, careers, and life journeys classmates have chosen to share.

Class Directory

Search by name, maiden or class name, city, or state. Contact cards may include phone, email, and mailing address when permission has been given.

Call, Text, Email, and View Story

On a phone, tap the available buttons to call, send a text, write an email, or open the classmate’s story.

Classmate Spotlight

Highlights a classmate on the Home page and encourages everyone to discover more class stories.

Birthday Spotlight

Recognizes classmates who have chosen to share a birthday occurring during the current month.

Who’s New

Shows recently added classmates or recently submitted stories.

Community and Class Life

Community Feed

A shared space for greetings, class news, announcements, and updates. The Home page also shows a preview of recent Community Feed posts.

Our Journey

Tells the history of the Class of 1976—from the early school years through graduation, life’s many journeys, the 50th reunion, and the years ahead.

Events

Shows approved upcoming and past class events, including reunions, celebrations, memorials, and gatherings.

Photos Gallery

Opens class and reunion photographs so classmates can revisit and share memories.

Legacy Garden

An uplifting place to honor classmates who have passed and preserve the memories they left with us.

Privacy

Phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, birthdays, and other personal information appear only when the classmate has chosen to share them.

Install ClassConnect on Your Phone

Installing creates a ClassConnect icon so you can open it like an app without searching for the web address.



iPhone or iPad

Use Safari
  1. Open the ClassConnect website in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button—the square with an upward arrow.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Keep the name ClassConnect, then tap Add.
  5. Find the ClassConnect icon on your Home Screen and tap it to open.

Do not see “Add to Home Screen”? Tap Edit Actions at the bottom of the Share menu and add it, or confirm the page is open in Safari rather than another browser.

🤖

Android Phone or Tablet

Use Chrome
  1. Open the ClassConnect website in Google Chrome.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the upper-right corner.
  3. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Tap Install or Add to confirm.
  5. Find the ClassConnect icon on your Home Screen or app list and tap it to open.

The wording can vary. Depending on the phone and Chrome version, the option may say “Install app,” “Add to Home screen,” or appear as an install icon in the address bar.

Navigation Tips

Bottom Navigation

On a phone, use the bottom buttons for Home, People, Community Feed, Journey, and Events.

Side Menu

Tap the menu icon to reach Class Directory, Where Are They Now?, Community Feed, Memories, Our Journey, Events, and Legacy Garden.
